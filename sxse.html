SXSE Wrapped Internship

This internship from my school allowed us students to intern at "spotify" to develop skills that would assist me in my future career goals. We were assigned with producing a "spotify wrapped" sort of page about our imaginative person's experience at the South By South East event in Richmond, VA. Each group member had to work on different team each week/sprint.

Sprint 1

This week, I was on the Marketing team and made 2 tiktok videos to promote SXSE event in RVA. These videos were meant to promote the event by showcasing types of programs the event will have.

Sprint 2

I was put on the development team this week. I was tasked with making changes to the already established website. I added functionality to the website and added additional features like making the website move left and right depending on where you click and moving when you press left and right keys.
